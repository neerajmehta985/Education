How to Prepare for Entrance Exams

  • JEE: Master NCERT, practice mock tests, focus on concept clarity
  • NEET: Focus on Biology, Chemistry, Physics; revise daily
  • CLAT: Practice legal reasoning, GK, and English comprehension
  • CUET: Understand domain subjects, follow NCERT, practice MCQs
